Information about Wearble Jazz

Wearable Jazz allows three Sensors to be connected and control three different sections of the app.

"transport tempo" controls how fast the instruments play in beats per minute.

The "Vol" control of each section controls how loud that section is.

Drums

The Drums function best when your Sensor is in Gyroscope mode.

"connected to:" changes which Sensor the Drums respond to

"Sensitivity" changes how much you need to move the Sensor to activate the drums.

The purple visualisation shows you which drum is being played.

Melody

The Melody functions best in Accelerometer mode, but try both!

"Probability" allows you to change the chance at which melody notes are played.

The purple visualization shows the Melodies activation.

Effects

As the Effects player you get to change how the entire ensemble sounds.

"None" is good for using wearable jazz without the effects.

"Filter" allows the Effects player to remove high or low frequency content from the ensemble.

"Echo" creates a repeating echo of the ensemble.

"Glitch" is a two stage glitch effect the makes the ensemble sound like it's frozen in time.

The purple visualization goes up and down along with your DJ effects.
